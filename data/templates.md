# Plan: Make Codebase Templates Marketing-Focused

## Context

The templates in `data/mario/templates/codebase/` are used by Mario's marketing presence mapping agents to generate `.planning/codebase/` documents when analyzing a brand's existing marketing. These templates use marketing-specific terminology, examples, tools, and patterns.

## Templates

### 1. `audience-insights.md` — Audience Insights
- Audience demographics, psychographics, and behavioral data
- Buyer personas with Jobs-to-Be-Done statements
- Customer language patterns and vocabulary
- Audience research methods and data sources

### 2. `brand-guidelines.md` — Brand Guidelines
- Brand voice attributes matrix (we are / we are not)
- Tone variation by context and channel
- Visual identity standards (colors, typography, imagery)
- Word lists (preferred vocabulary, banned terms)
- Messaging do's and don'ts

### 3. `channel-architecture.md` — Channel Architecture
- Active marketing channels and their roles
- Channel-specific content formats and posting cadence
- Cross-channel content flow and repurposing patterns
- Distribution and promotion workflows

### 4. `content-inventory.md` — Content Inventory
- Website pages with purpose and performance notes
- Blog content by category with top performers
- Email sequences (automated and recurring) with metrics
- Social media profiles and activity levels
- Ad campaigns with budgets and results
- Downloadable assets and lead magnets

### 5. `marketing-gaps.md` — Marketing Gaps
- Missing content by funnel stage (awareness, consideration, decision, retention)
- Channel gaps (channels not yet active or underutilized)
- Messaging gaps (audiences or objections not addressed)
- Technical gaps (tracking, deliverability, compliance)

### 6. `performance-metrics.md` — Performance Metrics
- Channel-specific KPIs and benchmarks
- Conversion rates by funnel stage
- Email engagement metrics (open rate, click rate, unsubscribe)
- SEO performance (rankings, organic traffic, domain authority)
- Ad performance (ROAS, CPA, CTR by platform)
- Content performance (traffic, engagement, conversion by piece)

### 7. `tools-and-platforms.md` — Tools and Platforms
- Marketing tools in use (CMS, ESP, analytics, CRM, etc.)
- Platform configurations and integration status
- Account access and team permissions
- Automation workflows and trigger logic

## Verification

After templates are used:
1. Read each generated file to confirm marketing-specific content
2. Verify no generic/code-focused references remain
3. Verify consistency with the strategy guide approach
